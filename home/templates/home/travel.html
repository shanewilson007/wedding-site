Popular Attractions

There are several good choices for quality and unique hotels in the South Florida area. I've comprised a little list of a few popular ones that you're welcome to book a room with. These are merely suggestions however.

First on our list, we have the JW Marriott. Located in the heart of Brickell, there's no shortage of activities within walking distance. It is where the groom and bride will be staying. We have a block of rooms available starting at $279 per night. Contact Leo or myself if you would like more information or would like to join our party.

Website | Reserve: 305-329-3500

Second, we have the historic Biltmore Hotel. Located in Coral Gables, this is the closest lodging between the ceremony and reception. The Biltmore boasts the largest hotel pool in the United States and one of the finest golf courses.

Website | Reserve: 855-311-6903

If you're looking to stay closer to the beach, another iconic hotel to consider is the Fontainebleau. Located right in South Beach, it's a popular hotel for tourists and beach-goers alike. The Fontainebleau also contains Club LIV, being named one of the hottest nightclubs in the city.

Website | Reserve: 800-548-8886

EAST is a hotel located downtown Miami in Brickell City Centre. Opening in 2016, EAST is branded as a luxary hotel with several restaurants and a rooftop bar.

Website | Reserve: 305-712-7000

Bulla Gastrobar

Our event is an adults only, black tie invited, evening cocktail party.

Bulla, pronounced {boo-yah}, is a Madrid inspired tapas bar, with a variety of entres ranging from smaller shared plates to larger paella dishes for the hungrier guest.
They also boast a nice selection of hand-crafted cocktails and an impressive wine list.
Ultimately, we feel like there is something here for everyone to enjoy.

If you're interested, feel free to check out the Menu before hand!
